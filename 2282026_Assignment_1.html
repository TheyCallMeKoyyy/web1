What Is UNAI?

Universitas Advent Indonesia (UNAI) Is a Higher Education Institution manage By the Seventh-day Adventist Church of The Western Union of Indonesia. The History of UNAI is That it Was Founded in Cimindi in 1929 which was originally called Opleiding School der Advent Zending. In The Past, This School was Founded for the education of the evangelists of the seventh-day Adventist Church.


Universitas Advent Indonesia Has 6 faculties:

faculty of Philosophy
faculty of Teacher Training & Education
faculty of Economics
faculty of Nursing
faculty of Mathematics and Natural Sciences
faculty of Information Technology
